Recipes

Siga(Beef) Tibs:

Ethiopian beef tibs,known as siga tibs, are an Ethiopian favorite, seasoned with berbere spice and spicy awaze paste and served with injera bread

Kitfo:

It consists of minced raw beef, marinated in mitmita (a chili powder-based spice blend) and niter kibbeh (a clarified butter infused with herbs and spices).

Doro(Chicken) Tibs:

Ethiopian chicken tibs,known as doro tibs, are an Ethiopian favorite, seasoned with berbere spice and spicy awaze paste and served with injera bread


Doro(Chicken) Wot:

Using the omnipresent mixture of berbere spices, a heavy load of Ethiopian butter, chicken, eggs, and onions, doro wat is born. The sauce is mostly made from onions that have been stewed down for so long, they disintegrate into a puree.

Beg(Lamb) Wot:

The meat iscut into tiny cubes, then stewed with a generous amount of red berbere seasoning, some extra cumin, onions, garlic, and a bit of tomato puree to make the sauce.

Alicha Wot:

The meat iscut into tiny cubes, then stewed with a small amount of tumeric seasoning,, onions, garlic, to make the sauce. Then topped off with some fresh Jalapeño peppers for flavor!


Vegatarian/Vegan


Misir Wot:

The lentils are cooked with a couple spoons of berbere spice powder to give them a nice redness in color, and cooked until tender, yet they still have some texture to them.

Ater Kik Alicha:

The lentils are cooked with a few spoons of turmeric powder to give them a nice yellow in color, and cooked until tender, yet they still have some texture to them. hen topped off with some fresh Jalapeño peppers for flavor!

Gomen:

These fragrant collards are cooked with an Ethiopian-style flavored with cardamom.hen topped off with some fresh Jalapeño peppers for flavor!
